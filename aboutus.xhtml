Welcome To Sweet Bloom Bakers!

About Us

We serve pastel pastries and dreamy drinks — all lovingly handcrafted with a sprinkle of magic. Every item on our menu is made from scratch, inspired by the joy of baking and the warmth of shared memories. It’s more than just a café; it’s a cozy, whimsical corner for sweet moments, pretty photos, and heartfelt connections.

Sweet Bloom Bakers began as a simple idea between four friends who shared a love for baking, storytelling, and beautiful food. What started as casual weekends in the kitchen—covered in flour and laughter—soon bloomed into a full-fledged dream. We poured our hearts into perfecting each recipe, capturing each creation through our lenses, and learning everything we could about the art of baking. Together, we turned our passion into a little slice of magic for others to enjoy.

Our journey is rooted in nostalgia—watching our mothers and grandmothers bake with love, scribbling down family recipes, and bringing our own twist to them. Now, we’re here to share those flavors with you. At Sweet Bloom, we believe in quality, creativity, and the beauty of baking from the heart. Whether it’s a flaky tart, a soft pastel macaron, or a glittering lavender latte, everything we serve is a labor of love.

We’re not just bakers—we’re bloggers, photographers, storytellers, and best friends. We hope Sweet Bloom becomes your go-to spot for comfort, joy, and inspiration. Thanks for being part of our story. Let’s keep making memories—one dreamy bite at a time.

At the heart of Sweet Bloom Bakers are four passionate women who turned their shared love for baking, creativity, and friendship into something truly magical. Together, we bring you pastel pastries, dreamy drinks, and an experience wrapped in warmth and wonder.

🍰 Najwa — The Founder & Heart of the Kitchen

Najwa is the soul behind Sweet Bloom. With a deep love for tradition and all things homemade, she dreamed of creating a space where people could slow down, savor beautiful bakes, and make sweet memories. From sketching out the very first menu to designing the café’s charming aesthetic, Najwa’s vision turned a simple idea into a cozy reality. She believes that baking is a language of love—and Sweet Bloom is her way of sharing it with the world.

📣 Syahirah — The Marketing Queen

Charming, clever, and full of fresh ideas, Syahirah is the voice that brings Sweet Bloom to life outside the kitchen. With her creative flair and storytelling magic, she connects us with our community through social media, events, and collaborations. She's the reason you stumbled upon our dreamy Instagram feed or heard about our seasonal specials. Syahirah makes sure every campaign, caption, and promo sparkles with our true essence: sweetness, sincerity, and a sprinkle of pastel charm.

🥐 Atiesya — The Confectioner Extraordinaire

From delicate sugar flowers to melt-in-your-mouth pastries, Atiesya is the artist behind every dessert masterpiece. She’s the one who turns everyday ingredients into edible art—think sugar cookies, chewy choco chips, and double choco chip blooms. With a background in culinary arts and an eye for detail, Atiesya adds magic and beauty into every bite. Her desserts don’t just taste amazing—they’re little love letters made of sugar.

☕ Sofea — The Barista with a Creative Twist

Sofea is our caffeine queen and flavor explorer. She crafts drinks that are as enchanting as they are delicious—from spanish to french vanilla choco drinks. Her attention to detail and love for experimenting with flavors mean that every cup she makes is full of personality. You’ll often find her humming to soft café music while swirling latte art with a smile. Sofea believes that a good drink should not only warm your hands but also your heart.
